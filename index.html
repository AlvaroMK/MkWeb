Description

Video games have always been a passion since I got my first console when I was only 7 years old. Since then I have had a clear goal: to dedicate myself to develop them and be able to contribute a small part of what they have been for me. This led me initially to study Computer Science, and later to specialize in Video Game Development.

Currently, I focus on not stop learning. I am interested in video game development from its most technical to its most creative side, being mainly a video game programmer but having also worked as a game designer or even as a 2D Artist. In general, I dedicate my free time to improve professionally and increase my portfolio, and lately I have been interested in VFX.
