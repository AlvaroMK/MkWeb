Information

  • Engine: Unity Engine

  • Status: Completed
  • Development: Feb 2022
  • Genre: Action-Adventure
  • Platforms:
    • Windows (itch.io)
    • MacOS (itch.io)

Summary

  • Game developed in just one week for the Brackeys' Game Jam 2022.1 following the theme 'It is not Real'.
  • You play as Gwen, a girl whose friend has allegedly disappeared inside an abandoned and terrifying mansion.
  • Aim your flashlight and kill the ghosts that will try to surround you, prevent your fear bar from reaching its maximum and advance room by room until you discover the dark mystery surrounding the house!

Contributions

We were two programmers on the project. My main work was:

  • Movement system: Both of the main character and some enemies.
  • Power-Ups system: Throughout the game you can find a series of consumables that improve your stats temporarily or permanently.
  • Enemy Spawn: In a parametrizable circumference around the character and outside the viewport limits.
  • Level design:
    • Design of all the rooms in the game.
    • Final battle design.
