Summary

  • Game developed in just two days for the Global Game Jam 2025 following the theme 'Bubbles'.
  • Beat the frantic bubble-related minigames that Pop it up offers you! You'll need a lot of imagination, patience and lungs to complete them. Enjoy!
    • Controls: mouse movement and blowing into the microphone (clicking the mouse doesn't work, it's intentional).
    • There are a total of five different minigames, each with its own mechanics.
  • Description of the minigames:
    • Bubble path: follow the bubble path with the mouse to the goal to pop them all. If all of them are red skulls or you don't have time, you lose.
    • Flappy bubble: Blow to give a timely boost to the man hanging from his gum. If he collides with any of the cutlery or the boundaries of the screen, you lose.
    • Shoot the bubble: Pop all the bubbles by aiming with the mouse and blowing at the right moment. If you shoot a bomb or run out of time, you lose.
    • The right size: Keep the bubble the right size when the time is up. If it ends and the bubble is not the right size, you lose.
    • Baby Bubble: Don't make noise! The little Bubble is sleeping. Avoid waking her up to win.
  • Winner of the award for best original idea by decision of the professional jury at the Complutense University of Madrid.

Contributions

For this Jam I was part of Design. My main work was:

  • Ideation and design of minigames:
    • Although the ideas for the mini-games came out of a general brainstorm of the team, in the end the final decision was taken by the design team.
    • The design of the bubble gum balloon mini-game, the bubble corridor and the bubble fps (shooter) fell to me.
    • Game flow.
    • Difficulty levels: Almost all mini-games have up to three difficulty levels, which increase with the achievement of the tests whether you pass them or not.
  • Art and visual design:
    • I was in charge of coming up with a visual identity of my own, one that worked and simple enough to be able to produce the graphics quickly and give time to iterate if something didn't fit.
    • About half of the assets are created by me.
