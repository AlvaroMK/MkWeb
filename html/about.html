About me

Hi there! I'm Alvaro Ajenjo, a programmer, designer, VFX artist and in general a videogame enthusiast. Since I got my first console when I was 7 years old I have not been able to separate myself from them, and over time I realized that they are my true passion. This led me to study Computer Engineering, and later a master's degree specialized in videogame programming, but I've also been learning a multitude of disciplines that can be applied to their development directly or indirectly: from making my first steps as a pixel artist to playing the guitar, writing narrative or being interested in audiovisual communication. Keep scrolling down to learn more about me!
